Full Stack Projects

Next Round's On Me

Social network where users can follow friends, check out local bars and restaurants, send friends 'rounds', and redeem 'rounds' sent to them. Architecture uses React, Express, Node.js, and Postgres. Technologies include Redux and AWS image hosting. All styling uses raw CSS3 to imitate Facebook styling (circa late 2020).

Codevous

Meetup clone focused on programmers. Architecture uses React, Flask, SQLAlchemy, and Express. All styling uses raw CSS3 to give a code editor / terminal feel.

Bike Around

AirBnB clone focused on renting bicycles. Architecture uses React, Express, Node.js, and Postgres.

What to Watch

Goodreads clone for movies. Architecture uses Pug, Express, Node.js, and Postgres.

About Chris

Chris has a passion for building things that make the world a better place. Whether bicycles, organizations, or software, he is commited to working with mission-driven organizations to develop solutions for complex problems facing the community. As a software developer, he brings a wealth of problem solving experience developed through years of designing programs and managing teams. Chris enjoys bicycling and going on adventures with his partner and two energetic puppers.
